# AI Equipment Notes

## Scope

Applies to files under `common/ai_equipment/`.

## Required Reference

Read `common/ai_equipment/AI_Equipment_Reference/ai_equipment_authoring_reference.md` before adding or changing AI equipment designs, target variant match values, unlock conditions, or persistence tracking.

## Operating Rules

- This mod does not rely on AI self-created or self-upgraded equipment variants; equipment designs are issued through scripted effects and related workflows.
- When adding a new AI equipment design, update the related design, scripted effect, decision or unlock condition, and any tracking documentation together.
- Preserve existing file encoding and line endings unless the user explicitly asks for conversion.
